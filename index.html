About me

I have graduated from Politehnica University of Bucharest with a Bachelor degree in Automatic Control and Computer Science. In comparison with the common association with Mechanical Engineering, the profile of my studies were significantly closer to Electrical Engineering and Computer Science. My Bachelor Thesis was an investigation on various control algorithms for modern electrical motors.

I followed my passion for Robotics and enrolled in the Systems & Control Master's Program at TU Delft in 2019. I got to learn from incredible professionals in the fields of Robotics, Artificial Intelligence, and various other branches of Applied Mathematics such as Control Theory, Graph Theory, and Numerical Optimization. My Master Thesis is related to Autonomous Drifting of automobiles, and it was a long marathon on system modeling, coding and optimization altogether.

During the last two years I developed a strong interest in Data Science and Data-centered problems in general. The upheaval of Machine Learning is well motivated by me, given that so many problems receive better solutions thanks to simply exploiting what collected data offers. I focused a significant portion of my time in extending my knowledge on adjacent subjects and practical tools used in the real world. I believe Data Science resonates well with my strenghts and affinities, and most of the theory behind it was already part of my background (Bachelor's and Master's curricula). I decided this is the direction worth my time and knowledge, and I seek to contribute to solving real problems with it.

Outside work, I have constructed a reading list which is too lengthy for my daily reading habit. I started a short while ago wood carving, or making ugly wooden miniatures while listening to audiobooks. I enjoy hiking and playing football, both replaced with a gym subscription during the pandemic. I like to spend lazy nights playing online video games or boardgames.
